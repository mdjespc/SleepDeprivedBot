Command List:

**announce** <channel> <message> - Sends an announcement.
**bitrate** <channel> - Returns the bitrate of a voice channel.
**ping** - Pings the bot and returns its latency.
**sm** <channel> <message> - Sends a message.
**se** <channel> <embed> - Sends an embedded message.
**echo** <phrase> - Echoes a message.
**help** - Lists all commands.
**kalek** - Praises Kalek.
